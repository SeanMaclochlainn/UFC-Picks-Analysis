Former Strikeforce middleweight championLuke Rockhold (12-2 MMA, 2-1 UFC) is the choice of all eight MMAjunkie writers and radio hosts over perennial contenderMichael Bisping (25-6 MMA, 15-6 UFC) in the UFC Fight Night 55 main event.

UFC Fight Night 55 takes place Friday at Allphones Arena in Sydney. The entire event streams on UFC Fight Pass.

The other three fights on the main card, though, nearly are unanimous.

In the co-main event, lightweightsAl Iaquinta (9-3-1 MMA, 4-2 UFC) andRoss Pearson (16-7 MMA, 8-4 UFC) will tangle. Six of our eight staff members like Pearson in the fight. Seven of eight likeClint Hester (11-3 MMA, 4-0 UFC) to beat Australia’sRobert Whittaker (12-4 MMA, 3-2 UFC), and seven of eight believe home favoriteSoa Palelei (21-4 MMA, 3-2 UFC) will topWalt Harris (7-3 MMA, 0-2 UFC) at heavyweight.

Check out the full picks above.

Additionally, leave your main-card picks below. As usual, MMAjunkie reader “IAMMA” is organizing the MMAjunkie Member Picks, where “Walter White” and “sadowolf” are our overall leaders in some closely contested categories.

For more on UFC Fight Night 55, check out the UFC Rumors section of the site.
